My History with Gaming

Gaming to me is something that I don't think I'll ever grow out of. Ever since I first started playing Super Mario on Gameboy, I've loved every minute of it throughout my life. For the most part, gaming is a part of my everyday routine, whether it it be by myself or with a bunch of my friends. Typically, I will play just about anything, just so long as it has a good story. Historically, I've played an mostly Nintendo and Sony consoles, but now I play on PC exclusively.

Some of my all time favorites include:

  • Pokemon Crystal - I love them all, this one is just my favorite.
  • Super Mario - Every mario game has been splendid.
  • Minecraft - Probably have around 6,000+ hours on this, and still going.
  • CoD: BO1 - Too many good times with my friends on this game, couldn't exclude it.
  • Halo 3 - Played this all the time with my cousin back in the day.
  • Undertale - Great story in such a short amount of time.
