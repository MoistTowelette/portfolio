My Passion for Music

My career with music started the summer before I got into middle school. I was going to join band, and my mom thought it would be a good idea to take piano lessons so I could get familiar with music beforehand. Well, it was defintely worth it, as I have played ever since and it has helped with understanding music and music theory to this day. Because of some pretty small choices I made years ago, I am now a Jazz Perfomance major at UWSP and am enjoying every day of it.

List of notable music accomplishments:

  • Made it to State Solo & Emsemble 3+ times.
  • Played in Capital Conference Honors Band
  • Got placed into the Jazz Emsemble at UWSP
  • Played with Emmy-nominated drummer Matt Wilson
  • Performed in multiple concerts at UWSP

Instruments I play:

  • Saxophone (Alto/Tenor) - 8+ years
  • Piano - 8.5 years
  • Clarient - 3 years
  • Voice - 2 years
  • Trombone/Drumset - 5 months each
